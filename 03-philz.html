Philz Coffee

The Fastest way to Order the World’s Slowest Coffee

Design & Direction, Work & Co.


This work was completed during my tenure at Work&Co's Brooklyn office. Partner Joe Stewart lead this work with myself, Corey Edmonson, Chris Nieri and more.

Background

With their cult-like following, theirlocations on Apple and Facebook campuses, investments by Snoop Dogg, and their lovable founder, Philz Coffee is unlike any beverage operation in America. Their cafés combine a lazy, dorm room aesthetic with fastidious (and time consuming) coffee creations. The result: the longest wait times ever experienced by coffee drinkers.
