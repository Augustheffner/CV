Background

With their cult-like following, theirlocations on Apple and Facebook campuses, investments by Snoop Dogg, and their lovable founder, Philz Coffee is unlike any beverage operation in America. Their cafès combine a lazy, dorm room aesthetic with fastidious (and time consuming) coffee creations. The result: the longest wait times ever experienced by coffee drinkers.
